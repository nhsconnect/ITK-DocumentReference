    The following structure definitions are available in this profile.

    NameTypeDescription & Constraints
    COFE-Document-Bundle-1BundleThis Bundle resource is constrained to represent a container to collect a combination of the COFE Document resources.
    COFE-DocumentReference-1DocumentReferenceProfile used for CDA on FHIR Document Reference
    CareConnect-Encounter-1EncounterCareConnect Encounter profile
    CareConnect-Patient-1PatientThe Patient resource carries the patient demographic details
    CareConnect-Practitioner-1PractitionerCareConnect Practitioner profile
    COFE-Related-Person-1RelatedPersonThis Related Person resource represents information for a person with a relationship with the patient
    CareConnect-Organization-1OrganizationCareConnect Organization profile
    COFE-Location-1LocationThe Location resource provides information and details on the physical location and the services provided.
    COFE-Device-1DeviceThis resource identifies an instance of a manufactured item that is used in the provision of healthcare without being substantially changed through that activity. The device may be a medical or non-medical device.
    COFE-Binary-1BinaryProfile to store base64 encoded binary.
